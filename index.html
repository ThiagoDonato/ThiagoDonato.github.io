Permutation Visualizer V3

What is this? (Click to expand)

This is an interactive tool for exploring permutations and their properties, specifically focusing on "2413" patterns. A permutation is just an ordered list of unique numbers. The graph visualizes your permutation, with the x-axis showing the position (index) and the y-axis showing the value.

A "2413" pattern is a subsequence of four elements whose values are ordered like the numbers 2, 4, 1, and 3. For example, in the permutation 2,4,5,1,3, the subsequence (2, 4, 1, 3) forms a "2413" pattern. The subsequence (2, 5, 1, 3) is another one. This tool found 2 such patterns.

How to use it

  • Manual Swapping: Simply click on any two points in the graph. They will swap places, and the tool will instantly update the count of 2413 patterns.
  • Optimizer Mode:
    1. Toggle "Optimizer Mode" on. The button will show "ON".
    2. Click on any number of points you want to re-order. They will turn green.
    3. Click "Apply Optimizer". The tool will arrange the selected values into a pre-computed optimal order that aims to maximize the number of 2413 patterns they can form.
